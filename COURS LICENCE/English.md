
##### 1 Mettre à la forme interrogative 
Did it cost a lot ? 
Did they have tea in the garden? 

##### 2 Répondre aux questions négativement suivant le modèle :
Eg : Did she feel happy ? (sad) $\implies$ No, she didn't, she felt sad.

No I didn't, I bought her a record.
No they didn't, they spoilt their daughter.

##### 3 Bâtir des phrases suivant le modèle 
Eg : We (to watch) a film on TV when the telephone (to ring) $\implies$ We were watching a film on TV when the telephone rang.

They were having a picnic when their dog ran away
I was waiting for Barbara when her brother arrived

##### 4 Répondre aux questions suivant le modèle :
Eg : Have you seen this film ? (last month) $\implies$ Yes, I have. I saw it last month.

Yes, I have. I finished it this morning. 
Yes she has. She went to England during the holidays. 

##### 5 Transformer les phrases suivant le modèle 
Eg : They are playing chess (+three hours). $\implies$ They have been playing chess for 3 hours.

Dad is working in the garden(+ an hour) $\implies$ Dad has been working in the garden for the past hour.
She is waiting for the bus (+ten minutes) $\implies$ She has been waiting for the bus for 10 minutes.

##### 6 Mettre le verbe  au prétérite ou au present perfect

I bought this record at the HMV shop. 
Look! I bought a new tie.

##### 7 Put in 'for' or 'since'

It's been raining since lunchtime
Tom's father has been doing the same job for 20 years.
Have you been learning English for a long time?

##### 8 Put the verb into the correct form, present perfect or past simple

I don't know where Amy is. Have you seen her ?
